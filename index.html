Batman

Batman is a crime fighting superhero. His name is Bruce Wayne and when he was a kid he watched both his parents get mugged and killed. Because of that he now spends his time fighting crime in the streets of Gotham City.

The Joker

The Joker is batmans arch-nemesis. He is a loose cannon whos only purpose or joy in life is to make Batman suffer. He doesn't have a super defined back story and it seems to change from version to version; but over all he seems to have been a stressed out guy that caught up in some crime, and fell into a vat of chemicals. Clasic comic book logic, he came out homicidal and looken like a clown.
